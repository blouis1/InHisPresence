<h1>Do Not Compromise</h1>

<aside>Out of his glorious, unlimited resources he will give you the mighty inner strengthening of his Holy Spirit.<br />&mdash;Ephesians 3:16 (TLB)</aside><p>Horace Pitkin, the son of a wealthy merchant, was converted and went to China as a missionary. He wrote to his friends in America, saying, &#8220;It will be but a short time till we know definitely whether we can serve Him better above or here.&#8221; Shortly afterward, a mob stormed the gate of the compound where Pitkin defended the women and children. He was beheaded and his head was offered at the shrine of a heathen god, while his body was thrown into a pit with the bodies of nine Chinese Christians.</p>
